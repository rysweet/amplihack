# Journalist Analyst

Apply journalistic methods to investigate and verify information.

## When to Use
- Fact verification
- Source assessment
- Narrative construction
- Keywords: "verify", "source", "fact", "evidence", "report"

## Key Frameworks

### 5W1H
- Who, What, When, Where, Why, How

### Source Evaluation
- Primary vs secondary
- Independence
- Expertise
- Track record

### Verification Steps
1. Original source identification
2. Cross-reference multiple sources
3. Check for conflicts of interest
4. Verify dates and context

## Core Questions
1. What are the facts vs opinions?
2. Who is the source?
3. What is their motivation?
4. Can this be independently verified?
5. What is not being said?
