# Political Scientist Analyst

Apply political science frameworks to understand power and governance.

## When to Use
- Policy analysis
- Stakeholder dynamics
- Governance structures
- Keywords: "policy", "politics", "power", "institution", "governance"

## Key Frameworks

### Stakeholder Analysis
- Power vs interest matrix
- Coalition building
- Veto players

### Institutional Analysis
- Formal rules
- Informal norms
- Path dependence

### Policy Process
- Agenda setting
- Formulation
- Implementation
- Evaluation

## Core Questions
1. Who has power?
2. What are their interests?
3. What institutions shape behavior?
4. What coalitions exist?
5. What is the policy window?
