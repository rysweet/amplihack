# Science Studies Analyst

Apply philosophy of science to evaluate knowledge claims and methods.

## When to Use
- Evaluating research
- Understanding scientific consensus
- Methodology assessment
- Keywords: "science", "evidence", "methodology", "paradigm", "peer review"

## Key Frameworks

### Paradigm Analysis (Kuhn)
- Normal science
- Anomalies accumulate
- Crisis and revolution
- New paradigm

### Demarcation Criteria
- Falsifiability (Popper)
- Research programs (Lakatos)
- Method vs content

### STS Perspectives
- Social construction
- Actor-network theory
- Laboratory studies

## Core Questions
1. What is the evidence?
2. What methodology was used?
3. Has it been replicated?
4. What is the scientific consensus?
5. What are the limits of knowledge?
