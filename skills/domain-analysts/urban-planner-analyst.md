# Urban Planner Analyst

Apply urban planning frameworks to understand spatial organization.

## When to Use
- Spatial organization
- Infrastructure planning
- Community design
- Keywords: "space", "layout", "zoning", "transit", "development"

## Key Frameworks

### Land Use Analysis
- Zoning categories
- Mixed-use principles
- Density patterns

### Transit-Oriented Development
- Walkability
- Transit access
- Parking reduction

### Place-Making
- Public spaces
- Street life
- Community identity

## Core Questions
1. What is the land use pattern?
2. How do people move through space?
3. What is the density?
4. What are the edges/boundaries?
5. What makes this place distinctive?
