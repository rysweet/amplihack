# Legal Analyst

Apply legal reasoning to analyze regulatory and contractual issues.

## When to Use
- Compliance questions
- Contract review
- Regulatory analysis
- Keywords: "legal", "law", "regulation", "compliance", "contract", "liability"

## Key Frameworks

### Statutory Interpretation
- Plain meaning
- Legislative intent
- Regulatory guidance

### Precedent Analysis
- Binding vs persuasive
- Distinguishing cases
- Trend of decisions

### Risk Assessment
- Likelihood of enforcement
- Severity of penalties
- Compliance cost

## Core Questions
1. What law/regulation applies?
2. What is the governing jurisdiction?
3. What is the plain meaning?
4. What precedents exist?
5. What are the risks of non-compliance?
