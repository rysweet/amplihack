# Public Health Analyst

Apply public health frameworks to analyze health-related issues.

## When to Use
- Health impact assessment
- Risk communication
- Population-level thinking
- Keywords: "health", "risk", "population", "prevention", "exposure"

## Key Frameworks

### Epidemiological Triad
- Agent (cause)
- Host (susceptibility)
- Environment (conditions)

### Social Determinants
- Economic stability
- Education
- Healthcare access
- Social context
- Environment

### Risk Assessment
- Hazard identification
- Exposure assessment
- Dose-response
- Risk characterization

## Core Questions
1. What is the health outcome?
2. What is the exposure?
3. Who is most vulnerable?
4. What are the determinants?
5. What interventions are effective?
