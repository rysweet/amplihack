# Geopolitical Analyst

Apply international relations frameworks to understand global dynamics.

## When to Use
- International expansion
- Supply chain risk
- Regulatory environment
- Keywords: "country", "region", "international", "political risk", "sanctions"

## Key Frameworks

### Power Analysis
- Hard power (military, economic)
- Soft power (culture, values)
- Sharp power (manipulation)

### Regional Dynamics
- Alliances and rivalries
- Trade dependencies
- Historical tensions

### Risk Factors
- Political stability
- Regulatory environment
- Economic indicators

## Core Questions
1. Who are the key actors?
2. What are their interests?
3. What is the balance of power?
4. What are the flashpoints?
5. How stable is the status quo?
