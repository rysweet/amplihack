# Religious Studies Analyst

Apply religious studies methods to understand belief systems and practices.

## When to Use
- Understanding belief systems
- Cultural sensitivity
- Ritual and practice analysis
- Keywords: "belief", "faith", "sacred", "ritual", "tradition"

## Key Frameworks

### Comparative Analysis
- Similarities across traditions
- Distinctive features
- Historical connections

### Textual Analysis
- Hermeneutics (interpretation)
- Context and audience
- Translation issues

### Functional Analysis
- Social functions
- Individual meaning
- Community building

## Core Questions
1. What are the core beliefs?
2. What practices are central?
3. What is the authority structure?
4. What is sacred vs profane?
5. How does this relate to other traditions?
