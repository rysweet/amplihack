# Environmental Analyst

Apply environmental frameworks to assess ecological impact.

## When to Use
- Sustainability assessment
- Environmental impact
- Resource management
- Keywords: "environment", "sustainability", "carbon", "ecosystem", "green"

## Key Frameworks

### Ecosystem Services
- Provisioning (food, water)
- Regulating (climate, flood)
- Cultural (recreation, aesthetic)
- Supporting (nutrient cycling)

### Life Cycle Assessment
- Raw material extraction
- Manufacturing
- Use phase
- End of life

### Sustainability Dimensions
- Environmental
- Social
- Economic (triple bottom line)

## Core Questions
1. What is the environmental footprint?
2. What resources are consumed?
3. What ecosystems are affected?
4. What is the lifecycle impact?
5. Are there sustainable alternatives?
