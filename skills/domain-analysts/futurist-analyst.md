# Futurist Analyst

Apply foresight methods to anticipate and prepare for future possibilities.

## When to Use
- Strategic planning
- Risk assessment
- Innovation direction
- Keywords: "future", "trend", "scenario", "anticipate", "emerging"

## Key Frameworks

### Scenario Planning
- Multiple plausible futures
- Key uncertainties as axes
- 2x2 scenario matrix

### Horizon Scanning
- H1: Current (0-2 years)
- H2: Emerging (2-5 years)
- H3: Transformational (5+ years)

### Weak Signal Detection
- Early indicators of change
- Fringe innovations
- Anomalies and outliers

## Core Questions
1. What trends are driving change?
2. What are the key uncertainties?
3. What weak signals exist?
4. What are the wildcards?
5. What's the preferred future?
