# Sociologist Analyst

Apply sociological frameworks to understand social structures and dynamics.

## When to Use
- Understanding group dynamics
- Analyzing inequality
- Social change analysis
- Keywords: "society", "social", "group", "inequality", "structure"

## Key Frameworks

### Structural-Functionalism
- Parts contribute to whole
- Equilibrium seeking
- Latent vs manifest functions

### Conflict Theory
- Power and inequality
- Competing interests
- Social change through conflict

### Symbolic Interactionism
- Meaning through interaction
- Identity construction
- Micro-level focus

## Core Questions
1. What social structures are involved?
2. Who benefits from the current arrangement?
3. What norms govern behavior?
4. What are the power dynamics?
5. How is meaning constructed?
