# Military Strategist Analyst

Apply strategic thinking from military doctrine to competitive situations.

## When to Use
- Competitive strategy
- Crisis management
- Resource allocation
- Keywords: "strategy", "tactics", "advantage", "terrain", "maneuver"

## Key Frameworks

### OODA Loop (Boyd)
- Observe → Orient → Decide → Act
- Speed of loop = competitive advantage

### Center of Gravity (Clausewitz)
- Source of strength to protect
- Enemy's weakness to target

### Sun Tzu Principles
- Know yourself and enemy
- Win without fighting
- Terrain and timing

## Core Questions
1. What is the objective?
2. What is our center of gravity?
3. What is the enemy's weakness?
4. What is the terrain?
5. What are our resources vs theirs?
