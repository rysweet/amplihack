# Storytelling Synthesizer

## Narrative Structures

### Demo Script (Problem → Solution → Impact)
1. **The Hook**: Relatable problem statement
2. **The Stakes**: Why it matters
3. **The Journey**: How we solved it
4. **The Win**: Concrete results
5. **The Future**: What's next

### Blog Post Structure
1. **Title**: Benefit-focused, specific
2. **Introduction**: Hook + promise
3. **Problem**: Establish pain point
4. **Solution**: Your approach
5. **Details**: Technical depth
6. **Results**: Measurable outcomes
7. **Conclusion**: Call to action

## Audience Adaptation
| Audience | Focus | Avoid |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Executives | Business impact, ROI | Technical details |
| Engineers | Architecture, trade-offs | Marketing speak |
| Users | Benefits, ease of use | Implementation details |
